Answering Door Bell

The Answering Door Bell is a simple device that displays a message to your guests at the door with just the click of a button. Any button of an IR remote can be programmed to display a particular message on the screen.

One of my best projects, the Answering Door Bell was initiated by the chairperson of our school. Though first prototype was made by our ATL incharge, Mr. Hirak Biswas, I am taking the project further. This simple device displays a message to the guest on your behalf if you are busy at that moment. This project was showcased at the Make Tomorrow For Innovation Generation (MTFIG) National Showcase, New Delhi (2018) and AIM (2021)

Problem statement
