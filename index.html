Some of my interesting projects

Listed below are some of my interesting projects. Most of them are completed at but their IOT version is stil under development. All the projects are aimed at removing some or the other grievances of our society.

Answering Door Bell

One of my best projects, the Answering Door Bell was initiated by the chairperson of our school. Though first prototype was made by our ATL incharge, Mr. Hirak Biswas, I am taking the project further. This simple device displays a message to the guest on your behalf if you are busy at that moment. This project was showcased at the Make Tomorrow For Innovation Generation (MTFIG) National Showcase, New Delhi (2018)

Water Overflow Detector / Smart Water Tank

This was my one of very first project made with an arduino and solves a very common problem of our society- shortage of water. The device can be installed on any overhead tank and then you don't have to manually operate the water pump. The device will do it for you, withou even a drop of water being wasted.

Electronic Voting Machine

This was just an hobby project completed on 19 May 2019 (the last voting day of the 17th Lok Sabha Elections), as a small honour to the most crucial aspect of the world's largest democracy.

Some of my Participations

I had an opportunity to participate in various district level competitions as well as a few bootcamps and national level competitions. Some of them are listed below

Make Tomorrow For Innovation Generation

Make Tomorrow For Innovation Generation (MTFIG) was the most prestigious competition I've ever attended. It was a three day workshop-cum-showcase held at New Delhi where top 50 participants from all over India competed to win a chance to participate in Maker Faire, San Mateo, USA.

Ideate for India

Ideate For India (IFI) was another such challenge organisied by the Department of Information and Technology in collaboration with Intel. I had a chance to participate in its workshop held at Kolkata where more than a hundred students participatre from 10 states of Easter India.

National Creativity Olympiad

I have participated in the National Creativity Olympiad twice (2017 and 2018). In 2017 our school was decleared the overall runner-up, leaving behind more than twenty other participating schools.

Other competitions

Apart from the above, I've also participated in various district and inter-school competitions, some of which include RoboZest (District level) organisied in our school, LEONARZO organisied at Rajendra Vidyalaya, Jamshedpur to name a few.
