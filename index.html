Participations and Achievments

MTFIG
Delhi

Make Tomorrow For Innovation Generation (MTFIG) was the most prestigious competition I've ever attended. It was a three day workshop-cum-showcase held at New Delhi where top 50 participants from all over India competed to win a chance to participate in Maker Faire, San Mateo, USA.

IFI
Kolkata

Ideate For India (IFI) was another such challenge organisied by the Department of Information and Technology in collaboration with Intel. I had a chance to participate in its workshop held at Kolkata and was one of the ten represenatatives from Jharkhand.

National Creativity
Olympiad

I have participated in the National Creativity Olympiad twice (2017 and 2018). In 2018 our school was decleared the overall runner-up, leaving behind more than twenty other participating schools.

Vacathon
DOE, Isreal (onine)

Vackathon was organised by Isreal's ministry of Education and hosted 100 students each from India , Isreal and China . The organisers had made teams of 7 to 10 students and our team - 3 Indians and 4 Isralis - stood third in innovation leaving 30 teams behind and won a audience's favourite.

AIM
Delhi (online)

Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) is a prestigious all India competition organised by the NITI aayog every year. In the 2020-21 session , I was one of the top 300 finalists and had a chance to attend the student entrupeurship programme organised in colllaboration with Adobe
